About TejasCare24

Compassion, Expertise, and Trust at Your Doorstep

Our Founding Principle

We aim to provide complete ICU facilities at home at an affordable cost, delivering compassionate services to patients who are suffering from various diseases and need special attention.

At TejasCare24, we believe that world-class healthcare should be accessible to everyone, right in the comfort of their own homes. We are dedicated to delivering professional care for all kinds of pre & post-operative patients, including those recovering from Neuro, Cancer, or Cardio-related conditions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our comprehensive services include experienced Doctors, certified Nurses, trained Attendants, expert Physiotherapists, and a wide range of medical equipment available for rent or purchase. We strive to create a healing environment that provides total comfort and peace of mind for both patients and their families.

Our Mission: To be the most trusted provider of home healthcare services in the Delhi NCR region, known for our clinical excellence, compassionate care, and unwavering commitment to our patients' well-being.
